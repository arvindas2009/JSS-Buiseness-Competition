Rising Vision Problems

Over 2.2 billion people worldwide have vision impairment, and 1 in 3 teenagers now rely on corrective eyewear due to excessive screen exposure and poor eye health.

Traditional Glasses don't Heal

Traditional glasses don’t heal — they only correct temporarily: Regular eyewear simply compensates for weak vision instead of improving it. The eye becomes dependent, leading to worsening power over time.

Laser surgery isn’t for everyone

While laser surgery can restore vision, it’s expensive, invasive, and not suitable for every eye type — especially those with corneal or medical limitations.

Sustainability gap:

The eyewear industry produces millions of tons of plastic waste every year. Frames are made of non-biodegradable materials, contributing to environmental pollution.

Lack of personalization:

Traditional eyewear offers limited customization — users have to adapt to their glasses, not the other way around.

PRODUCT

Opticious Glasses are AI-powered smart eyewear that automatically adjust their lenses in real time to correct vision and help naturally improve eyesight within months. Connected to the Opticious App via Bluetooth, they track eye health, screen time, and habits while offering personalized improvement plans. Built with adaptive fiber lenses, sustainable biodegradable frames, and long-lasting lithium batteries, they combine innovation with eco-friendliness. The glasses include features like blue light and UV protection, an AI custom frame builder for personalized design, a 24×7 virtual eye-care assistant, and real-time cloud-based vision monitoring — delivering comfort, intelligence, and style in one pair.

OUR JOURNEY

Smart Vision Assessment:

When a user first gets their Opticious Glasses, the companion Opticious App performs an AI-powered eye test. It analyzes vision patterns, eye strain, and focusing ability in real-time using adaptive algorithms.

Personalized Calibration:

The glasses feature adaptive fiber lenses whose focal length automatically adjusts based on the user’s needs. This is controlled through micro-sensors and AI calibration from the app, ensuring crystal-clear focus at all times.

Continuous Vision Training:

As the user wears the glasses, the AI system gradually optimizes and trains the eye muscles by slightly adjusting focus levels over time. This natural process helps reduce eye power steadily within 6–12 months.

Sustainable Energy System:

The glasses are powered by a miniature lithium-ion battery, similar to those used in earbuds. One 3–4 hour charge lasts an entire week, ensuring smooth operation with minimal environmental impact.

AI Frame Customization:

Users can design frames through the AI Custom Frame Builder, which scans facial features to recommend frame shapes that are ergonomic, stylish, and biodegradable — aligning with both comfort and sustainability.

Real-Time Monitoring & Updates:

The Opticious App tracks eye health, usage hours, and vision improvement, sending personalized feedback and reminders — making it a complete AI-driven eye care ecosystem.
